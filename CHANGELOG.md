# Artemis II Progress Bar Changelog

## [Unreleased]

## [0.0.1]
### Added
- Pixel-art Artemis II SLS rocket progress bar replacing IntelliJ's default
- Determinate mode: rocket travels left to right toward the Moon
- SRB separation animation: boosters drift outward and fade from 50% to 75% progress
- Indeterminate mode: rocket loops continuously left to right
- Animated starfield with twinkling stars
- Scrolling diagonal stripe fill showing progress
- Moon anchored at the right edge of the bar
